> This document outlines the structure and content for the VIP/Rewards email flow. This flow is designed to recognize and reward your most loyal customers, making them feel appreciated and encouraging their continued business.

# VIP/Rewards Flow: [CLIENT NAME]

**Flow Goal:** To acknowledge and reward high-value customers, increasing their loyalty and lifetime value.

**Trigger:** When a customer meets the criteria for VIP status (e.g., based on lifetime spend, number of orders, or manual addition to a VIP list).

**Target Audience:** Your most valuable customers.

---

## Flow Structure & Timing

This flow can be a single email or a series, depending on the structure of your VIP program.

| Email # | Timing | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Immediately upon reaching VIP status | Welcome to the VIP Program |
| **2** | Ongoing | Exclusive VIP Perks & Early Access |

---

## Email 1: Welcome to the VIP Program

*   **Timing:** Immediately after a customer qualifies for the VIP program.
*   **Goal:** To officially welcome the customer to the VIP program and outline their new benefits.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "You're in! Welcome to the [Brand Name] VIP Club."
*   **Headline:** "Welcome, VIP!"
*   **Body:**
    *   Congratulate the customer on reaching VIP status.
    *   Clearly list the exclusive benefits they now have access to (e.g., free shipping on all orders, exclusive discounts, early access to new products).
    *   Make them feel special and valued.
*   **CTA:** "Explore Your VIP Perks"

---

## Email 2: Exclusive VIP Perks & Early Access

*   **Timing:** Ongoing, as new products are launched or special promotions are run.
*   **Goal:** To continuously provide value to your VIPs and keep them engaged.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "Your exclusive early access is here" or "A special gift for our VIPs"
*   **Headline:** "Just for our VIPs..."
*   **Body:**
    *   Provide VIPs with early access to new product launches or sales.
    *   Send them exclusive content or special offers not available to other customers.
    *   Reinforce their status as a valued member of your community.
*   **CTA:** "Shop Now" or "Claim Your Gift"

---

## Klaviyo Implementation Notes

*   **VIP Segment:** Create a dynamic segment in Klaviyo that automatically adds customers who meet your VIP criteria. This will be the trigger for the flow.
*   **Exclusive Content:** Use conditional logic in your campaigns to show or hide certain content blocks for your VIP segment.
*   **A/B Testing:**
    *   Test different VIP benefits to see what resonates most with your top customers.
*   **Smart Sending:** Enable Smart Sending for this flow.
