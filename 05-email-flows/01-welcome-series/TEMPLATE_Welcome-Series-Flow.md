> This document outlines the structure and content for the Welcome Series email flow. This is a critical flow for making a strong first impression, introducing the brand, and converting new subscribers into first-time buyers.

# Welcome Series Flow: [CLIENT NAME]

**Flow Goal:** To welcome new subscribers, introduce them to the brand, and encourage their first purchase.

**Trigger:** When someone subscribes to the email list (e.g., through a popup, footer form, or at checkout).

**Target Audience:** All new email subscribers who have not yet made a purchase.

---

## Flow Structure & Timing

This flow consists of 3-5 emails sent over a period of 7-10 days. The timing is designed to keep the brand top-of-mind without overwhelming the new subscriber.

| Email # | Timing | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Immediately | Welcome & Deliver Offer |
| **2** | 1-2 days after Email 1 | Brand Story & Mission |
| **3** | 2-3 days after Email 2 | Social Proof & Best-Sellers |
| **4** | 2-3 days after Email 3 | Overcome Objections & Build Trust |
| **5** | 1-2 days after Email 4 | Final Reminder of Offer |

---

## Email 1: Welcome & Deliver Offer

*   **Timing:** Immediately after signup.
*   **Goal:** To deliver the promised incentive (e.g., discount, free shipping) and make a strong first impression.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "Welcome to [Brand Name]! Here’s your 10% off."
*   **Headline:** A warm and enthusiastic welcome message.
*   **Body:**
    *   Thank the subscriber for joining.
    *   Clearly present the discount code or offer.
    *   Briefly introduce the brand and what makes it special.
    *   Showcase a few stunning product images.
*   **CTA:** "Shop Now & Use Your Code"

---

## Email 2: Brand Story & Mission

*   **Timing:** 1-2 days after Email 1.
*   **Goal:** To build a deeper connection by sharing the brand's story, mission, and values.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "Here’s what we’re all about..."
*   **Headline:** Introduce the founder or the "why" behind the brand.
*   **Body:**
    *   Tell the brand's origin story.
    *   Share the brand's mission and core values.
    *   Use authentic, personal language.
    *   Include a photo of the founder or the team.
*   **CTA:** "Learn More About Our Mission"

---

## Email 3: Social Proof & Best-Sellers

*   **Timing:** 2-3 days after Email 2.
*   **Goal:** To build trust and guide the subscriber towards a purchase by showcasing popular products and customer testimonials.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "See what everyone’s loving..."
*   **Headline:** "Our Community Favorites"
*   **Body:**
    *   Highlight 3-5 best-selling products with high-quality images.
    *   Include 2-3 powerful customer reviews or testimonials (with photos if possible).
    *   Remind them of their welcome offer.
*   **CTA:** "Shop Best-Sellers"

---

## Email 4: Overcome Objections & Build Trust

*   **Timing:** 2-3 days after Email 3.
*   **Goal:** To address common questions and concerns that might be preventing a purchase.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "Got questions? We’ve got answers."
*   **Headline:** Address a common pain point or question directly.
*   **Body:**
    *   Create a mini-FAQ section addressing common objections (e.g., shipping, returns, product quality).
    *   Highlight key trust-builders like satisfaction guarantees, free returns, or customer service contact information.
    *   Reinforce the benefits of the products.
*   **CTA:** "Shop with Confidence"

---

## Email 5: Final Reminder of Offer

*   **Timing:** 1-2 days after Email 4.
*   **Goal:** To create a sense of urgency and drive the first purchase before the welcome offer expires.

### Content:

*   **Subject Line:** "Last chance for 10% off!"
*   **Headline:** "Your Welcome Offer is Expiring Soon"
*   **Body:**
    *   Clearly state that this is the final reminder for their exclusive offer.
    *   Use urgency-driven language.
    *   Showcase a few more products or a compelling lifestyle image.
    *   Keep the copy short and to the point.
*   **CTA:** "Claim Your Discount Now"

---

## Klaviyo Implementation Notes

*   **Flow Filter:** Exclude anyone who has placed an order since starting this flow.
*   **A/B Testing:**
    *   Test different discount amounts in Email 1 (e.g., 10% vs. 15% vs. free shipping).
    *   Test different subject lines for each email.
    *   Test the timing between emails.
*   **Smart Sending:** Enable Smart Sending for this flow to avoid sending emails too close together if the subscriber is also in other flows.
