## Key Statistics & Benchmarks

### Revenue Performance
- **Email automations (flows)** generate up to **30x more revenue per recipient (RPR)** than one-off campaigns
- **Abandoned cart flows** have the highest RPR of any automation: **$3.65 average**
- Standard email campaigns generate only **$0.11 RPR** on average
- **Welcome flows** generate **$2.65 per recipient** on average
- Top 10% of welcome flows achieve a **10.53% placed order rate**

### Engagement Metrics
- **Post-purchase emails** have the highest open rate: **61.68%**
- **Browse abandonment emails** generate an average click rate of **5.48%**

### Strategic Benefits
1. **Higher revenue per recipient** - Automated flows dramatically outperform campaigns
2. **Personalized messages at scale** - Works 24/7, engaging subscribers 1:1 at every customer journey stage
3. **Improved customer experience** - Addresses common customer service requests automatically

## The 10 Essential Email Automations

### 1. Welcome Series Emails
**Trigger:** Someone first subscribes to email list  
**Goal:** Introduce brand and convert new subscribers into buyers  
**Performance:** $2.65 RPR average, top 10% achieve 10.53% placed order rate

**Best Practices:**
- Use gorgeous product shots
- Clear list of product benefits
- Introduce founder/brand story
- Include discount offer (typically 10%)
- Remember: email signup â‰  SMS consent
- Can incorporate both email and SMS for dual opt-ins

**Example Brand:** Otherland (candles) - combines product imagery, benefits, founder intro, and 10% discount

---

### 2. Abandoned Cart Recovery Emails
**Trigger:** Item placed in cart but purchase not completed  
**Goal:** Re-engage shoppers to complete purchase  
**Performance:** $3.65 RPR average (highest of any automation)

**Best Practices:**
- Personalization is essential
- Vary messaging based on cart value
- Only offer discounts for high-value carts to avoid wasting resources
- A/B test subject lines and discount offers
- Balance urgency and persuasion

**Example Brand:** Montirex (sportswear) - varies cart recovery messaging by item value, generates 30% of Klaviyo-attributed revenue

---

### 3. Browse Abandonment Emails
**Trigger:** Online store visitors leave without making a purchase  
**Goal:** Nudge people back to website to browse or complete purchase  
**Performance:** 5.48% average click rate

**Best Practices:**
- Send after shopper spends enough time looking at a product
- Include "You may also like" section with complementary products
- Use dynamic content based on location or preferences
- Extend online browsing behavior into email

**Example Brand:** Charlotte Stone (shoes) - uses product recommendations to continue browsing experience

---

### 4. Post-Purchase Transactional Emails
**Trigger:** Order placed, order fulfilled, shipping updates  
**Goal:** Enhance communication, build trust, lay groundwork for repeat purchases  
**Performance:** 61.68% open rate (highest among all flows)

**Types:**
- Order confirmation
- Shipping confirmation
- Delivery notifications
- Production status updates (for custom/made-to-order products)

**Best Practices:**
- Understand compliance laws around transactional vs. marketing emails
- Align with brand visual aesthetics
- Include key information: timelines, order number, prices, tracking link
- Can add marketing elements if you have consent
- For complex production processes, provide frequent status updates

**Example Brands:**
- **Brightland** (olive oil) - simple series with brand-aligned aesthetics, founder signature
- **Manly Bands** (custom wedding rings) - automated production status updates via Google Sheets + Klaviyo API integration

---

### 5. Product Review Requests
**Trigger:** Post-purchase, after customer has received and used product  
**Goal:** Collect social proof, increase trust, boost conversions

**Best Practices:**
- Time request appropriately (after delivery + usage time)
- Make review process simple and frictionless
- Consider incentives for reviews
- Use reviews in future marketing materials

---

### 6. Replenishment Reminders
**Trigger:** Time-based, after typical product usage period  
**Goal:** Remind customers to reorder consumable products

**Best Practices:**
- Calculate average usage cycle for products
- Personalize based on purchase history
- Make reordering seamless (one-click if possible)
- Ideal for supplements, skincare, pet food, etc.

---

### 7. Back-in-Stock Notifications
**Trigger:** Previously out-of-stock product becomes available  
**Goal:** Capture demand from interested customers

**Best Practices:**
- Require explicit opt-in for notifications
- Send immediately when product restocks
- Create urgency (limited quantity messaging)
- Include product details and easy purchase path

---

### 8. Instructional Emails
**Trigger:** Post-purchase, for complex products  
**Goal:** Ensure customer success, reduce returns, increase satisfaction

**Best Practices:**
- Provide setup guides, tutorials, tips
- Use video content when possible
- Address common questions proactively
- Build confidence in product usage

---

### 9. VIP Customer Exclusive Emails
**Trigger:** Customer reaches VIP/loyalty tier  
**Goal:** Reward best customers, increase lifetime value

**Best Practices:**
- Offer exclusive access to products, sales, content
- Make customers feel special and appreciated
- Provide early access to new launches
- Consider special discounts or perks

---

### 10. Birthday and Anniversary Emails
**Trigger:** Customer birthday or purchase anniversary  
**Goal:** Personal connection, drive repeat purchase

**Best Practices:**
- Collect birthday data during signup
- Send special offers or discounts
- Personalize messaging
- Track purchase anniversaries for milestone celebrations

## Critical Implementation Notes

### Email Campaigns vs. Automations
- **Campaigns** are still vital for:
  - Promotions and limited-time offers
  - Messages for wider, segmented audiences
  - Seasonal announcements
  
- **Automations** excel at:
  - Behavioral triggers
  - Personalized 1:1 messaging
  - 24/7 engagement
  - Higher revenue per recipient

### Automation Benefits Summary
1. Sent automatically based on real-time actions
2. Timely, targeted, and relevant
3. Work around the clock
4. Generate revenue while you sleep
5. Scale personalization efforts
6. Free up team for strategic work
