### How to Use This Document

1.  **Plan Your Quarter:** At the beginning of each quarter, use the **Testing Calendar** to plan the tests you want to run. Prioritize tests based on the PIE framework (Potential, Importance, Ease).
2.  **Create a Test Plan:** For each test in the calendar, create a detailed test plan using the appropriate template (e.g., `TEMPLATE_Subject-Line-AB-Test.md`).
3.  **Run Your Test:** Execute the test in Klaviyo according to your plan.
4.  **Log Your Results:** Once the test is complete, create a new entry in the **A/B Test Log**. Summarize the key details, results, and learnings.
5.  **Share Your Learnings:** Regularly review the test log with your team to share insights and build a collective understanding of what works for your audience.
