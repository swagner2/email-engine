> This template provides a guide to creating and optimizing a Klaviyo preference center. A preference center is a powerful tool for collecting zero-party data and giving your customers more control over their experience.

# Data Collection Template: Preference Center

---

## 1. Description

A preference center is a page where subscribers can manage their communication preferences. It allows them to choose what kind of content they want to receive, how often they want to receive it, and on which channels.

---

## 2. Pros and Cons

| Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Reduces Unsubscribes:** Giving users more control can reduce your overall unsubscribe rate. | **Requires Maintenance:** You need to ensure your preference center is always up-to-date and that your segments are correctly configured. |
| **Rich Data Collection:** You can collect a wide range of data on customer preferences. | **Lower Engagement:** Not all subscribers will take the time to update their preferences. |
| **Improves Customer Experience:** A preference center shows that you respect your customers and are committed to sending them relevant content. | |

---

## 3. Best Use Cases

*   **Reducing Unsubscribes:** Instead of a one-click unsubscribe, direct users to a preference center where they can opt-down instead of opting-out.
*   **Improving Engagement:** By sending more relevant content, you can improve your open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates.
*   **Segmenting Your Audience:** A preference center is one of the best ways to build highly targeted segments based on customer interests.

---

## 4. What to Include in Your Preference Center

### Communication Frequency

*   Allow users to choose how often they want to hear from you (e.g., once a week, once a month).
*   Consider a "pause" option for users who want to take a temporary break from your emails.

### Content Preferences

*   Allow users to choose what kind of content they are interested in (e.g., new product announcements, sales and promotions, blog posts, company news).

### Product Category Preferences

*   Allow users to choose which product categories they are interested in. This is especially important for brands with a wide range of products.

### Channel Preferences

*   Allow users to choose which channels they want to hear from you on (e.g., email, SMS).

### Personal Information

*   Allow users to update their personal information, such as their name, email address, and birthday.

---

## 5. Optimization Tips

*   **Promote Your Preference Center:** Don't just link to it in your email footer. Actively promote it in your welcome series and in ongoing campaigns.
*   **Make It Easy to Use:** Your preference center should be clean, simple, and easy to navigate.
*   **Show the Value:** Explain to users why they should update their preferences. How will it benefit them?
*   **Use Dynamic Content:** Use the data you collect in your preference center to create dynamic content in your emails.
*   **Keep It Up-to-Date:** As you add new product categories or content types, be sure to update your preference center.
*   **Build It in Klaviyo:** Klaviyo has a built-in preference center that is easy to set up and customize.
