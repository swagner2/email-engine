## Section 3: Tone Variation

Tone is the application of your voice in different situations. How does your brand's tone shift to fit the context of the message?

| Scenario | Tone to Adopt | Example Snippet |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Welcoming a new subscriber** | Enthusiastic, helpful, inviting | "We're so excited to have you! Here's a little something to get you started..." |
| **Announcing a new product** | Confident, exciting, informative | "It's finally here! Get ready to experience..." |
| **Handling a customer complaint** | Empathetic, respectful, solution-oriented | "We're so sorry to hear about your experience. Let's make this right." |
| **Abandoned Cart Reminder** | Gentle, helpful, slightly urgent | "Did you forget something? Your cart is waiting for you!" |
| **Celebrating a milestone** | Joyful, grateful, communal | "We couldn't have done it without you! To celebrate..." |
