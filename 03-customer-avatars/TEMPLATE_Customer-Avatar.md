## Section 2: Psychographics

This section explores the customer's personality, values, and lifestyle.

### 2.1. Goals & Motivations

*   **Primary Goal:** What is the main goal they want to achieve that relates to your product?
*   **Secondary Goals:** What are their other aspirations in life?
*   **Motivations:** What drives them to make decisions? (e.g., status, security, self-improvement)

### 2.2. Challenges & Pain Points

*   **Primary Challenge:** What is the biggest obstacle they face that your product helps solve?
*   **Secondary Challenges:** What other frustrations do they experience in their daily life?
*   **Fears & Anxieties:** What are they afraid of? What keeps them up at night?

### 2.3. Values & Beliefs

*   What is most important to them in life? (e.g., family, career, health, sustainability)
*   What are their core beliefs?

### 2.4. Hobbies & Interests

*   What do they do for fun?
*   What are their passions outside of work?
*   What kind of media do they consume? (e.g., blogs, podcasts, magazines, social media platforms)

## Section 3: Behavioral Insights

This section focuses on their purchasing habits and interactions with brands.

### 3.1. Purchasing Behavior

*   **Shopping Habits:** How do they prefer to shop? (Online, in-store, mobile)
*   **Purchase Drivers:** What factors influence their purchasing decisions? (e.g., price, quality, brand reputation, reviews)
*   **Deal Breakers:** What would prevent them from buying from a brand?

### 3.2. Brand Interactions

*   **Preferred Communication Channels:** How do they like to hear from brands? (Email, social media, SMS)
*   **Social Media Usage:** Which platforms are they most active on?
*   **What They Look For in a Brand:** What qualities do they value in the brands they support? (e.g., authenticity, transparency, customer service)

## Section 5: Marketing Message

Based on the information above, craft a core marketing message for this avatar.

*   **Elevator Pitch:** In 1-2 sentences, how would you describe your product to this person?
*   **Key Selling Points:** What are the top 3 benefits that will resonate most with them?
*   **Objection Handling:** What are their likely objections, and how can you address them proactively?
