> This document is a critical tool for translating product specifications into compelling marketing messages. Customers don't buy features; they buy the benefits those features provide. This matrix will serve as a central repository for your product messaging.

## How to Use This Matrix

1.  **List Your Products:** Start by listing your key products or product categories.
2.  **Identify Features:** For each product, list its key features. These are the factual, technical aspects of the product.
3.  **Translate to Benefits:** For each feature, ask "So what?" What does this feature *do* for the customer? This is the benefit.
4.  **Find the Emotional Hook:** Go one step further and ask, "How does that benefit make the customer *feel*?" This is the emotional driver behind the purchase.

## Key Takeaways for Marketing Copy

*   **Headlines:** Use the emotional hooks to write compelling headlines.
*   **Body Copy:** Use the benefits to explain the value of the product.
*   **Bullet Points:** Use the features to provide technical specifications for those who want them.
