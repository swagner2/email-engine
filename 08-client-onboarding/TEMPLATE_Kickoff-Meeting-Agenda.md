> This agenda provides a structure for the initial kickoff meeting with a new client. The goal of this meeting is to align on goals, review the initial discovery information, and set expectations for the project.

# Client Kickoff Meeting Agenda: [CLIENT NAME]

**Date:** `YYYY-MM-DD`
**Attendees:** `[List of attendees]`

---

## 1. Introductions (5 minutes)

*   Team introductions (both agency and client side).
*   Briefly review the purpose of the meeting.

## 2. Project Goals & Objectives (10 minutes)

*   Review the client's primary business goals for the email marketing program.
*   Translate those goals into specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) KPIs.
*   **Discussion Point:** What does success look like for you in 6-12 months?

## 3. Brand & Customer Deep Dive (25 minutes)

*   Review the `TEMPLATE_Brand-Research-and-Discovery.md` document that the client filled out.
*   Ask clarifying questions to gain a deeper understanding of:
    *   The brand's story and values.
    *   The target customer avatars.
    *   The competitive landscape.
*   **Discussion Point:** What is the single most important thing we should know about your customers?

## 4. Strategy Overview (10 minutes)

*   Present a high-level overview of the proposed email marketing strategy, including:
    *   Key email flows to be implemented.
    *   The approach to segmentation.
    *   The plan for email campaigns.

## 5. Process & Timeline (5 minutes)

*   Walk the client through the onboarding process using the `TEMPLATE_Client-Onboarding-Checklist.md`.
*   Outline the expected timeline for strategy, setup, and implementation.

## 6. Next Steps & Action Items (5 minutes)

*   Recap the key decisions and action items from the meeting.
*   Define the next steps for both the agency and the client.
*   Schedule the next check-in meeting.
